The application allows European Go players to instantly calculate their new Go rating depending on the given conditions. It allows to fetch current ratings from European Go Database.

The EGF rating system is derived from ELO rating system used by World Chess Federation (FIDE). You can find more information about rating and formulas on EGD website.

GoR Calculator was created to provide a mobile alternative to European Go Database's GoR calculator.
